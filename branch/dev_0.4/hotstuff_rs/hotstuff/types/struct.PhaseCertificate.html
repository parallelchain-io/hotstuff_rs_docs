Expand description

Cryptographic proof that at least a quorum of validators have voted for a Proposal or Nudge with the included chain_id, view, block, and phase.

Used to extend a block in the HotStuff subprotocol, and to optimistically advance to a new view in the pacemaker subprotocol.
